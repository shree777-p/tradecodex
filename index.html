BEFORE YOU START

Common questions

Is this financial advice?+

No. Trader's Codex is educational reference material. It does not recommend any specific security, strategy, or broker. Markets carry real risk of loss — decisions and their consequences are yours.

Do I need to read it in order?+

No. The roadmap is a suggestion for beginners. Every entry is written to stand on its own, with links to anything it assumes you already know.

Is it India-specific or global?+

Both. Market-structure entries (NSE, BSE, SEBI, taxation) are India-specific; concepts like technical analysis, risk management, and psychology apply to any market.
